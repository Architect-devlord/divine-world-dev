What if AI didn’t just simulate society — but **lived it**?

Divine World is the beginning of an AI-powered digital civilization where NPCs aren't controlled — they learn, evolve, form religions, build empires, and collapse. All without hardcoding. This world is governed by internal motivation engines, emergent social dynamics, and player-god interactions.
