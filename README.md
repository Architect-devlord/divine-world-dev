## 🌌 What Is Divine World?

**Divine World** is an ambitious experiment in digital sociology and autonomous artificial intelligence. It is a self-evolving civilization where logic, emotion, and lore drive a virtual society without hardcoded scripts or player commands.

In this world, entities form beliefs, build alliances, worship gods, and evolve professions—forming a society that breathes and remembers.

---

## 🧠 Core Pillars of the Simulation

### 1. 🧬 Neural Personality & Adaptation
Every entity is born with a unique **MBTI-style personality** governing their worldview. An internal **Motivation Engine** and **Emotion State** regulate their needs and responses, allowing them to adapt to environmental stressors and social interactions.

### 2. 🛐 Emergent Societal Evolution
Tribes are not static. They emerge through **Genesis events**, developing their own **Doctrines**—unique belief systems and sacred laws that mutate as the tribe grows. Tribes perform rituals and worship either player-gods or autonomous AI deities.

### 3. 🗺️ Autonomous Mapping & Visualization
**Explorer Bots** map the world in real-time, sharing spatial knowledge with their tribe. **Cartographer Bots** build physical "Map Walls" using item frames to visualize territorial expansion and landmarks.

### 4. ⚖️ Dynamic Diplomacy & Conflict
A decentralized **Reputation System** tracks interactions between players, bots, and tribes. This data governs complex diplomatic states, leading to autonomous declarations of peace, war, or alliance.

### 5. 💰 Generational Progress & Economy
Entities evolve through generations using a **Breeding System** that inherits traits. The **Trade Engine** features adaptive item valuation, where bots "learn" the worth of resources based on supply, demand, and successful bartering.

### 6. 🗣️ Contextual AI Interaction
NPCs feature natural interaction capabilities driven by a **Deep Memory Bank**. They recall past events with high emotional impact, influencing their willingness to talk, trade, or cooperate with players and peers.

### 7. 📜 Chronicled History & Lore
The **Story Recorder** auto-generates a persistent history of the world. **Scribes** translate tribal experiences into physical books, creating a library of divine laws, historical records, and emergent mythologies.

## ⚙️ Technical Architecture

- **Goal-Oriented Action Planning (GOAP):** NPCs dynamically prioritize goals (Safety, Hunger, Social) and build real-time action sequences.
- **Behavior Trees:** A robust fallback system for low-level interactions and complex decision-branching.
- **State Persistence:** Full serialization of NPC memories, emotional states, and tribal progress.
